What is RCA?

Root Cause Analysis — find the cause, not just the symptom

RCA is a systematic method for identifying the underlying cause of an incident — not just the visible symptoms. By finding the root cause, companies can prevent similar incidents from happening again.

Widely used in manufacturing, oil & gas, energy, and production facilities worldwide.

Features

Everything your team needs for structured RCA

RCA Tool guides engineering teams through a consistent, auditable investigation process.

Evidence Collection

Guided data entry for incident records, equipment history, and physical evidence. Keeps all investigation data in one place from day one.

Why Tree Analysis

Build structured Why Tree diagrams to map cause-and-effect chains systematically — drilling down from the visible symptom to the true root cause.

Corrective Action Planning

Define corrective and preventive actions with responsible owners, due dates, and status tracking — ensuring findings lead to real improvements.

Automatic PDF Report

Generate a professional, formatted investigation report in one click — ready to present to plant management or include in audit documentation.
